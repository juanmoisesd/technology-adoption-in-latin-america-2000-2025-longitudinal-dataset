### New Open Dataset Published: Longitudinal dataset (2000-2025) on digital technology adoption indicators across 21 Latin American and Iberian countries. Variables: internet penetration, mobile subscriptions, smartphone adoption, social media usage, e-commerce, digital payments, A

**Universidad Internacional de La Rioja (UNIR)** — Juan Moisés de la Serna Tuya (ORCID: https://orcid.org/0000-0002-8401-8018) has published a new open dataset: *Longitudinal dataset (2000-2025) on digital technology adoption indicators across 21 Latin American and Iberian countries. Variables: internet penetration, mobile subscriptions, smartphone adoption, social media usage, e-commerce, digital payments, A*.

The dataset is freely available under the **CC0 1.0 Universal** license, allowing unrestricted use, distribution, and reproduction.
