# Summary: Longitudinal dataset (2000-2025) on digital technology adoption indicators across 21 Latin American and Iberian countries. Variables: internet penetration, mobile subscriptions, smartphone adoption, social media usage, e-commerce, digital payments, A

**Author:** Juan Moisés de la Serna Tuya  
**Institution:** Universidad Internacional de La Rioja (UNIR)  

---

## What is this dataset?

Longitudinal dataset (2000-2025) on digital technology adoption indicators across 21 Latin American and Iberian countries. Variables: internet penetration, mobile subscriptions, smartphone adoption, social media usage, e-commerce, digital payments, A

## Why does it matter?

This dataset contributes to open science by making research data freely available for reuse, analysis, and citation.

## Who can use it?

- Researchers and academics
- Policy makers
- Journalists and science communicators
- Students and educators
- General public

## How to use it?

1. Download the data from [GitHub](https://github.com/juanmoisesd/technology-adoption-in-latin-america-2000-2025-longitudinal-dataset)
2. Open with Excel, R, Python or any data analysis tool
3. Cite using the CITATION.cff or CITATION.bib files
